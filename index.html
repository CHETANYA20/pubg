ABOUT PUBG MAPS

Sanhok is a new map that was introduced into the game on 6/22/2018. Different from the previous Erangel and Miramar map that was 8x8, Sanhok is a 4x4 sized map. Smaller map, bigger fights!

The chances for engaging opponents will be much higher so it will make for some high intensity battles.
